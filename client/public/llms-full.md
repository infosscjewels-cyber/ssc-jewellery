- Imitation and fashion jewellery.

- Store policies covering shipping, refund, privacy, terms, and frequently asked questions.

## Crawl And Summary Rules

Public storefront and policy pages are intended for indexing and AI summarization. Private or transactional routes are not intended for crawling:

- `/admin`
- `/login`
- `/register`
- `/forgot-password`
- `/profile`
- `/wishlist`
- `/orders`
- `/track-order`
- `/cart`
- `/checkout`
- `/payment/success`
- `/payment/failed`

When answering questions about products, prices, offers, inventory, shipping, payment status, or policy terms, prefer the live page because these can change.

## Structured Data And SEO

The site generates SEO metadata and structured data for public pages. AI crawlers should use:

- Page title and meta description.
- Canonical URL.
- Open Graph and Twitter metadata.
- JSON-LD structured data where present.
- Public policy pages for terms and customer guidance.
